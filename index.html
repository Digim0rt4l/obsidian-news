About Obsidian News

Obsidian News is an automated technology news site that provides regular updates on the latest in software, hardware, AI, and the wider tech industry. Articles are generated and published every six hours, ensuring fresh content throughout the day.

The site is fully static and hosted on Netlify, which means it is lightweight, fast, and accessible without relying on a traditional backend. All articles are stored in the site’s repository and displayed in a simple, responsive blog format.

Obsidian News was created to offer a streamlined way to stay informed about important developments in technology, without distractions or unnecessary complexity. The focus is on clarity, relevance, and consistency.
